About Me

Welcome to my website! I am a passionate Master's student at the University of Pennsylvania, specializing in Robotics. Currently, I am working as a Research Assistant at the Figueroa Robotics Lab, affiliated with the GRASP Lab at UPenn. I hold a Bachelor's degree in Mechanical Engineering from Mumbai University.

My primary areas of interest within robotics are Perception, Robot control, and motion planning. These subjects have captivated my curiosity, and I am eager to forge a career dedicated to exploring and advancing these fields. I am driven by a relentless passion for pushing the boundaries of technology and contributing to the ever-evolving realm of robotics.

During my leisure time, I find solace in listening to music, and I enjoy engaging in sports such as cricket and badminton. Please don't hesitate to connect with me; I'm always open to new opportunities and conversations.

Thank you for visiting my website, and I look forward to sharing and exploring the exciting world of robotics together!

Projects

Control of CrazyFlie quadrotor

Implemented geometric nonlinear controller for stable control of CrazyFlie quadrotor under Dr. M. Ani Hsieh's mentorship. Developed an obstacle free path using A* algorithm and implemented minimum snap trajectory on it. Incorporated State Estimation through Visual Inertial Odometry (VIO) and Error State Kalman Filter (ESKF).

NeRF: Neural Radiance Field in 3D Vision

Implemented Neural Radiance Fields to render a 3D model of a lego model and successfully obtained a PSNR of 25.17 under the guidance of Prof. Kostas Daniilidis.

Grand Theft Autonomous (GTA)

Developed an ESP32-S2-based autonomous robot with pushing capabilities and employs HTC Vive for localization and IR frequency detection under the guidance of Dr. Mark Yim. Achieved the distinction of being the runner up in the GTA competition hosted at the UPenn.

Pick and Place Challenge

Implemented path planning for 7 DOF Franka Panda Arm using Forward and Inverse Kinematics for stacking static and dynamic blocks where ROS was used as framework and Gazebo as visualization tool under the guidance of Dr. Cynthia Sung.

Design of Rover (Capstone Project)

Designed and manufactured a 6-wheeled robot with the capabilities of semi-autonomous navigation using stereo vision for collision prevention, remote sensing, and data transmission using under the supervision of Dr. Sangita Bansode and Prof. Arati Phadke.

Team Onyx India

Designed empennage for RC aircraft as Structural Lead for Aero Design Competition East - Advanced Class in Texas, USA (May 2022). Manufactured aircraft for Aero Design Competition East - Regular Class in Florida, USA (March 2020). Conducted aeromodelling workshops and introductory sessions for engineering students with Team Onyx India.
